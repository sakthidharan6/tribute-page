MS DHONI

About him

  • Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket.
  • He was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand), India.
  • MS Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor on the field, exceptional captaincy skills, and finishing abilities as a batsman, particularly in the limited-overs formats.
  • He made his debut for the Indian cricket team in December 2004 in an ODI against Bangladesh.
  • Dhoni served as the captain of the Indian national cricket team from 2007 to 2016.
  • ICC World Twenty20 in 2007.
  • ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
  • ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.
  • Dhoni was known for his unflappable temperament on the field, earning him the nickname "Captain Cool."
  • Dhoni captained the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the Indian Premier League (IPL)
  • IPL 2010 Title
  • IPL 2011 Title
  • IPL 2018 Title
  • IPL 2021 Title
  • IPL 2023 Title
  • IPL 5 Title Trophy
  • 12 IPL Playoff Out of 14 Season
  • 10 IPL Finals out of 14 Season
  • Dhoni was an exceptional wicketkeeper, known for his lightning-fast reflexes and efficient stumpings.
  • Dhoni has an iconic helicopter shot has become synonymous with his batting style.
  • He stepped down as the captain of the limited-overs formats in early 2017.
  • MS Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket in August 2020.
  • However, he continued to play in domestic T20 leagues.
  • Beyond cricket, MS Dhoni has also been involved in various business ventures and has served in the Indian Army as an honorary Lieutenant Colonel.
